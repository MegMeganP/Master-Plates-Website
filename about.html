What is Menu Engineering?

Menu Engineering is the art and science of perfecting your menu, down to the finest detail, in order to maximize customer satisfaction and your restaurant's profits. At Master Plates Menu Consultants, our experts scour all corners of the local market for data to create unique dishes, along with combining leading industry standards to streamline your process. We do it all, from assessing your customer base, analyzing local and accessible ingredients, beautify the plate aesthetics, print eye-catching menus, and train your staff to efficiently produce your menu items with the highest quality in mind. We create fulll menus, seasonal dishes, signature dishes, and tweak old favorites. We work with restaurants that are still in the conceptual phase, as well as established restaurants. We can help provide new staff, train existing staff, and help procure ingredients, supplies and equipment. We do everything with the goal of helping you to provide the best menu and tastiest dishes that your customers will ever experience.
